Why iota?


An iota is something of an extremely small amount. That's the idea behind iota's method for storing links. 8 characters, no apps, no bulk. Social bookmarking for the rest of us.

iota is for the times when you're using a work or school computer and want to save links, but don't have the option to install separate apps or browser add-ons. With iota, you don't need extra tabs, just log in once, and save links directly from your url bar
